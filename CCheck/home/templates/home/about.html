About CopyChecker

CopyChecker is one of the assignment plagiarism checking application which helps you to detect plagiarism between two assignment selected or offered by the authorized teacher. Assignment verification is the main concern here. It checks the authenticity of an assignment between two assignments offers by the authorized teacher with just a couple of clicks. This web application works with common text submition.Students just submit the assignment which is asked for. When the teacher checks for the plagiarism verification the CopyChecker application checks the authenticity and shows the percentage of the similarity between two assignments or texts within default comparison. The CopyChecker application is quite faster and confirm accuracy in the algorithm part. Therefore, the application mainly for the plagiarism detection of different subject's assignment of students by the teachers.
